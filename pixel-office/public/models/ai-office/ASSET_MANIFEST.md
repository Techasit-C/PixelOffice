# AI Office Model Asset Pack

Procedural low-poly/isometric 3D model assets inspired by the AI office concept.

## Formats

- `.glb`: recommended for Three.js, Babylon.js, Godot, Blender import, and web previews.
- `.obj` + `.mtl`: compatibility format for older tools.

## Included models

- `office_room_complete.glb` / `office_room_complete.obj`
- `desk_with_monitors.glb` / `desk_with_monitors.obj`
- `desk.glb` / `desk.obj`
- `monitor.glb` / `monitor.obj`
- `office_chair.glb` / `office_chair.obj`
- `server_rack.glb` / `server_rack.obj`
- `robot_assistant.glb` / `robot_assistant.obj`
- `whiteboard.glb` / `whiteboard.obj`
- `printer.glb` / `printer.obj`
- `storage_shelf.glb` / `storage_shelf.obj`
- `plant.glb` / `plant.obj`
- `water_cooler.glb` / `water_cooler.obj`

## Notes

- These are real 3D model files, not PNG images.
- Style is low-poly voxel/isometric, suitable as a starter asset pack.
- The full room is in `office_room_complete.glb`; reusable pieces are exported separately.
